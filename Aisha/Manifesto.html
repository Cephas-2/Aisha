Action Plan for Kilifi County

A Roadmap for Development, Growth, and Prosperity

Infrastructure Development

Upgrade roads, bridges, and transport systems; improve water supply and sanitation; expand electricity access in rural areas.

Education

Build and equip schools, provide scholarships, support teacher training, and enhance quality education programs.

Healthcare

Improve hospitals and health centers, ensure access to medicines and maternal care, and introduce mobile clinics for remote areas.

Agriculture & Food Security

Support small-scale farmers with tools and training, promote irrigation projects, and create markets for local produce.

Economic Growth & Jobs

Promote youth and women entrepreneurship, attract investment, and develop tourism opportunities along the coast.

Governance & Transparency

Ensure accountability in public funds, engage citizens in decision-making, and promote inclusive governance.

Environment & Sustainability

Promote tree planting, waste management, environmental conservation, and protect coastal and marine resources.
