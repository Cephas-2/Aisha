Early January

Road Construction

Road infrastructure is the backbone of development. Good roads connect our farmers to markets, our children to schools, and our families to hospitals. My commitment is to construct and upgrade all-weather roads across Kilifi County, ensuring safe, reliable, and affordable transport that opens up economic opportunities, creates jobs, and brings services closer to the people. When roads work, development moves.
